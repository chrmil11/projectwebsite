About IIT Rocket League

IIT Rocket League

What is IIT Rocket League?

IIT Rocket League is a group for IIT students to come together to casually and competitively play some fun rocket-powered car soccer. Created as a team under the Scarlet Computer & Console Gaming club, the IIT Rocket League team was initialized in the Fall 2017 semester. Starting out as a trio of students searching for others to play with, the team is slowly training to compete at a higher level.

The group is registered as the 51st chapter in the College Carball Association.

The CCA

What is the CCA?

The College Carball Association, also known as the CCA, is a college community-driven organization targeting university Rocket League players to come together to represent their school, hang out, practice, and compete in intense, friendly intercollegiate competition.
