IIT Scarlet Hawks Rocket League

Illinois Tech Rocket League

was formed in September 2017 within the Scarlet Computer & Console Gaming club. The group is registered as the 51st chapter in the College Carball Association.

What is the CCA?

The College Carball Association, also known as the CCA, is a college community-driven organization targeting university Rocket League players to come together to represent their school, hang out, practice, and compete in intense, friendly intercollegiate competition. If you're interested, stop by their Discord channel, Twitter, and Twitch accounts. They're pretty cool.
